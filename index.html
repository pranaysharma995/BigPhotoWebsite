#1 HOTEL IN MEXICO

Our Suites

The Suites of the Hacienda de San Antonio reflect the rich traditions of Mexico’s arts and crafts. Each Suite is individually decorated by some of the country’s leading artisans, creating unique spaces with their own fascinating stories.
The three Grand Suites offer sumptuous light filled rooms that are unequaled anywhere in the Americas.

Living

The Hacienda’s richly detailed rooms provide a sumptuous backdrop to life at the Hacienda. The Yellow Bar, with its views to the garden, is the social hub for evening cocktails and lively conversation. The wood beamed library is a quiet retreat while the light filled Mirador room is ideal for reading or a movie. The imposing Club Room with its volcanic stone columns is a place for dining, gathering or special events.

ACTIVITIES

Activities abound in our mild mountain climate. Enjoy horseback riding, mountain biking, hiking, birdwatching, nature walks, or leisurely strolls through our formal gardens within the grounds of the Hacienda. Lounge at our 110′ swimming pool, have a spirited game of tennis with our tennis pro or simply relax with a good book on the scenic Mirador terrace..
